Pariah is a stealth-narrative indie game in which the player takes on the role of a queer teenager attempting to escape a conversion therapy camp.

Pariah is being developed by a solo developer based in Portland, Oregon. The game seeks to give voice to an experience that is still happening to young people across the United States, using the unique empathy-building power of interactive media to make that reality impossible to ignore.
